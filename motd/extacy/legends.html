Legends:
Legends last for the length of the map or until you leave the server.

  • Arch_Angel - The Sentry King
    • Build 5 sentries
    • Build 2 dispensers
    • Gain 10 metal a second
  • Tokey - The Lightning God
    • Hurt players to charge your damage counter.
    • Damage counter is shown in top left of screen.
    • Once you have enough, press tab while looking at an enemy to fire your lighting.
    • The lighting bolt will hit other nearby enemies. Amount of damage done will be divided depending on how many enemies are in the chain.
        For example:
        500 charge and 2 enemies = 250 damage each
        900 charge and 3 enemies = 300 damage each
