Skills:
Skills have different levels. The more you buy, the better the skill is.
Skills will last until the end of the map, or until you leave the server.

Heal per secGives you 1 hp per second per level.
HP Spawn BufferSpawn with extra health. Slowly decreases over time.
SpeedRun faster (Now works with Chargin Targe).
GravityDecreases gravity. Lets you jump higher and farther.
Random KritzIncreases your chance of receiving kritz.
Mirrow DamageReflects 5% damage per level done to you onto your enemy.
Multi JumpLets you jump multiple times in mid air.
StealthIncreases your invisibility. Remove hats for increased effectiveness. Press taunt to see what you look like.
