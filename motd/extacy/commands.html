Player Commands:
All current commands are say commands. Press "Y" to open say menu.

menuOpens main menu. Purchase your skills and items here.
top10Shows top 10 cash holders. Press any number to close.
rankShows your rank compared to other players in cash ammount.
helpShows help menu. A description of all skills and items can be found here (Not yet completed)
!preferenceUsage: "!preference <TEAM>" - Tells the team-switch plugin which team you prefer to be on.
rtvRocks the vote. If enough people rtv then a map change vote will take place.
statsmeShows cash made/spent an hour, skills bought, K/D ratio and server cash stats.
